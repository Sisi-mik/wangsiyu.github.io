I am Wang Siyu, a

forth year PhD student at the Insititute of Linguistics and Applied linguistics, Peking University. I am also a member of Peking University Syntax Reading Group and Peking University Second Language Acquisition Lab.

I do theoretical and descriptive research on syntax and morphology usually involving Mandarin Chinese and its dialects, with an emphasis on argument structure and event structure. I am also interested in viewing Chinese from a cross linguistic perspective and do comparative research. I am currently working on my PhD thesis on the encoding of telicity in Mandarin Chinese.

Besides studying syntax, I also enjoy watching musicals, K dramas, K-pop, visiting museums and singing.
